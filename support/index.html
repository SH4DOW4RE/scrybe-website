Support

Get help with Scrybe.

Use this page for support with the Scrybe extension and the hosted Shadoweb backend. Self-hosted deployment work is outside the supported scope.

Support scope

Included Extension bugs, browser compatibility issues, pairing and recovery problems, sync behavior, and hosted backend availability.
Included Questions about privacy, account recovery, disconnecting browsers, or changing the backend URL in the extension.
Not included Deploying, debugging, securing, or maintaining Scrybe on hardware or infrastructure that Shadoweb does not control.

Request support

This form opens an email draft with the details you provide. Do not include recovery codes, device secrets, private keys, or sensitive browsing content.

Support is provided for the extension and Shadoweb-controlled backend only. Requests to deploy or administer Scrybe on private hardware may be declined.
